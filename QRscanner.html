How to Use This QR Scanner

  1. Click the "Start Scanner" button to activate your camera for scanning.
  2. Position one of the QR codes from your spreadsheet within the scanning frame.
  3. The scanner will automatically detect and decode the QR code.
  4. Scanned codes will appear in the "Scan Results" section and be added to the list below.
  5. Click "Stop Scanner" to turn off the camera when finished.
  6. Use "Clear Results" to reset the scanner and clear all scanned codes.

Note: Your spreadsheet contains QR codes for values 0001 to 0026. This scanner will detect these codes and display the corresponding values (e.g., 0001, 0002, etc.).
